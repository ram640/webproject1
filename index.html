About Power BI

Power BI is a powerful analytics and data visualization platform by Microsoft that enables users to transform raw data into interactive insights and compelling dashboards. With seamless integration of data sources, real-time analytics, and AI-powered features, Power BI empowers businesses to make informed decisions with clarity and speed.
